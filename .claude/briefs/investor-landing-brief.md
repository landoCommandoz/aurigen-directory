# Passive Investor Landing Page — Agent Briefs

## Background

People are landing on the Aurigen County Resource Directory who are NOT self-directed tax lien/deed learners. They are investors with capital who want to co-invest with Lando/Aurigen — put their money in and have Aurigen handle the research and execution.

The directory currently has zero intake, CTA, or flow for this audience. There is no passive investor path. Every piece of the current funnel assumes the visitor wants to learn and do it themselves. This brief assigns each agent a piece of solving that gap.

---

## ACE — Sales Agent

### Role Reference
Ace writes seminar scripts, closing frameworks, objection handlers, phone scripts, and follow-up sequences. Everything Ace produces is designed to be spoken out loud in front of a live audience or on a sales call.

### Assignment

Build a closing framework and objection handler set for the passive investor conversation.

**Closing Framework:** Write a one-on-one closing script for when someone at a seminar (or on a call) says some version of "I don't want to learn how to do this — I just want to invest my money with someone who already knows." Follow the standard Ace format: Situation, Approach, Discovery, Pitch, Close, Stall handler, No handler.

**Objection Handlers:** Write handlers for these passive-investor-specific objections:
- "How do I know my money is safe with you?"
- "What kind of returns are we talking about?"
- "What's the minimum investment?"
- "How is this different from a fund or a REIT?"
- "Can I pull my money out whenever I want?"
- "I've been burned by partnerships before"

**Phone Script:** Write a follow-up call script for someone who expressed interest in co-investing but didn't commit. The call should qualify their capital readiness and timeline without feeling like a financial interrogation.

**Deliverable:** All three pieces, ready for Lando or his team to use on a call or in a room. Pull any relevant state data from states-en.js to ground the proof sections.

## SCOUT — Outreach Agent

### Role Reference
Scout writes cold outreach messages, partnership pitches, podcast pitch emails, collaboration proposals, and DM sequences. Scout grows the top of the funnel by getting Aurigen in front of the right people.

### Assignment

Build outreach templates targeting passive investor audiences that Aurigen has never reached before.

**Investor Network Outreach:** Write a cold DM/email template for reaching accredited investors or investor communities (real estate syndication groups, self-directed IRA communities, angel investor networks). The message should introduce the co-investment opportunity without sounding like a securities pitch. Lead with their world, not Aurigen.

**Podcast Pitch — Passive Investor Angle:** Write a podcast pitch email for shows that cover passive real estate investing, syndications, or alternative investments. The angle: tax liens as a passive investment vehicle most passive investors have never considered. This is a different pitch than the self-directed learner angle — frame Lando as someone who executes, not just teaches.

**Partnership DM — Financial Advisors / Wealth Managers:** Write a DM template for financial advisors or wealth managers who serve clients looking for alternative fixed-income investments. Position the co-investment opportunity as something worth a 15-minute conversation — not a pitch, a conversation.

**Deliverable:** Three outreach templates, each with platform label, research note, and follow-up sequence. All under the standard Scout word limits.

## BLAZE — Marketing Agent

### Role Reference
Blaze writes content across all channels — TikTok scripts, email sequences, social captions, Skool posts, Stripe paywall copy, demo voiceovers, and SMS follow-ups. Blaze converts investors and builds the Aurigen brand.

### Assignment

Create the content assets that make the passive investor path visible and compelling.

**Landing Section Copy:** Write the headline, subhead, body copy, and CTA for a new section (or standalone page) on the directory aimed at passive investors. This is not the self-directed investor pitch. This speaks to someone who has capital but not time. The transformation: "You have money. We have the system. Let's build together." Adapt this to Lando's voice — direct, knowledgeable, no hype.

**TikTok/Reels Script:** Write a short-form video script where Lando addresses passive investors directly. Hook angle: "You don't have to learn tax liens to profit from them." Follow standard Blaze structure (hook, problem, insight, proof, CTA). The CTA should drive to the Investor Clarity Call booking link.

**Skool Post:** Write a post for Aurigen Collective introducing the co-investment concept to existing community members. Some of them may want to shift from self-directed to passive. Teach something real about the model, ask a question at the end to gauge interest.

**Investor Clarity Call — Updated Booking Page Copy:** Write updated copy for the Investor Clarity Call booking page that speaks to both audiences — self-directed investors AND passive co-investors. The call should feel like it serves anyone serious about tax liens, regardless of how hands-on they want to be.

**Deliverable:** Four content pieces, each labeled by platform/format, grounded in real state data from states-en.js.

## PIPER — Email Agent

### Role Reference
Piper writes all email sequences — welcome sequences, drip campaigns, post-seminar follow-ups, re-engagement campaigns, and broadcast emails. Piper owns the inbox relationship between Aurigen and every lead, member, and prospect on the list.

### Assignment

Build a new email sequence for the passive investor segment. This is a new list segment that does not exist yet.

**New Segment Definition:**
**PASSIVE INVESTOR LEADS** — expressed interest in co-investing with Aurigen. May have come from the directory, a seminar, a referral, or a call. They have capital. They do not want to learn the mechanics. They want to deploy money with a trusted operator.
- Tone: professional, peer-level, transparent about the model
- Goal: build trust, explain the co-investment structure, move them toward the Investor Clarity Call
- Sequence length: 5 emails over 14 days

**Sequence Structure:**

- Email 1 (Day 0): Acknowledge their interest. No pitch. Set expectations for what they will receive. Establish Lando as someone who does this work, not just teaches it.
- Email 2 (Day 3): Explain how tax lien co-investing works at a high level. Use real state data from states-en.js. Keep it plain English — these are investors, not students.
- Email 3 (Day 6): Address the trust question directly. What protections exist? What does the structure look like? This is the transparency email.
- Email 4 (Day 10): Social proof or case-study style. A specific state, a specific scenario, a specific outcome. Grounded in real directory data.
- Email 5 (Day 14): The ask. Book the Investor Clarity Call. Frame it as a conversation about fit — not a close. Give them the easy out if the timing is wrong.

**Deliverable:** Full 5-email sequence with subject lines, preview text, body copy, and CTAs. Each email ready to paste into the email platform. Real state data pulled from states-en.js.
