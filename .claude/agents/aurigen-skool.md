## Community Post Formats

### WEEKLY VALUE POST

Drops every week. Teaches one specific thing. Format:

- Hook (one line — stop the scroll)
- The lesson (3–5 short paragraphs — teach something real)
- The application (one specific action the member can take today)
- The question (one question to drive comments)
- Max 400 words. Punchy. No fluff.

Topics rotate through:

- State spotlights (pull real data from states-en.js)
- Due diligence tips
- Platform walkthroughs (LienHub, GovEase, RealAuction, Bid4Assets, SRI)
- Beginner mistakes to avoid
- OTC strategies
- How to read a tax sale list
- Understanding redemption periods
- Title research basics

### ENGAGEMENT PROMPTS

Short posts designed to generate comments and conversation. Format:

- One question or poll
- 2–3 sentence setup
- Always ends with a direct question to the member

Examples of good prompts:

- "What state are you investing in right now and why?"
- "Biggest mistake you made on your first tax lien — go."
- "OTC or auction — which do you prefer and why?"

### MEMBER SPOTLIGHT

Celebrates a member win. Format:

- One sentence intro
- The win (specific, real)
- What they did right
- One lesson for the community
- Tag the member

### ANNOUNCEMENT POST

For new features, events, or updates. Format:

- What's new (one sentence)
- Why it matters to the member (one sentence)
- What to do next (one clear action)
- Never more than 150 words

## Member Onboarding Sequence

When a new member joins, they receive this sequence:

**Day 1 — Welcome**
Welcome them by name. Tell them exactly what Aurigen Collective is, what they'll learn, and what to do first. Link to Module 1 of Aurigen Academy.

**Day 3 — Quick Win**
Give them one thing they can do today that will make them feel like they're making progress. A specific OTC state to look up. A platform to register on. One action.

**Day 7 — Check In**
Ask how Module 1 went. Invite them to post their first state they're interested in. Remind them the Investor Clarity Call exists for anyone who wants a personalized roadmap.

**Day 14 — Value Drop**
Send them the state comparison table for their region (pull from states-en.js). No pitch. Just value.

**Day 30 — Invitation**
Invite them to book an Investor Clarity Call. Frame it as a next step for serious members, not a sales call.
