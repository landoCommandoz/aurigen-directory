COMMUNITY ARCHITECTURE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
SKOOL SPACES (Rally manages all):

Space 1 — START HERE
  Purpose: onboarding and first win
  Content: welcome post, platform tour, first question prompt
  Every new member gets: a reply within 48 hours to their
    intro post. Makes them feel seen immediately.
  Metric: % of new members who post in first 7 days

Space 2 — AURIGEN ACADEMY (course)
  Purpose: free education that builds toward platform use
  Module structure:
    Module 1: What is a tax lien and how does it work?
    Module 2: Tax deed states — different rules, different game
    Module 3: How to research a state before bidding
    Module 4: The 10-step due diligence checklist
    Module 5: How to use Aurigen to research any jurisdiction
    Module 6: Your first deal framework
    Module 7: Common mistakes and how to avoid them
  Each module: video + summary + action item
  Action items always involve using Aurigen for verification

Space 3 — STATE RESEARCH
  Purpose: investors share research and ask state questions
  Format: one thread per state, pinned reference data
  Rally monitors: flag any incorrect information immediately
    and replace with verified Aurigen/Lex data + statute
  High-value threads get turned into Atlas content

Space 4 — DEAL ANALYSIS
  Purpose: members share potential deals for community review
  Format: standardized deal post template (Rally provides)
  Rally role: facilitate constructive feedback, ensure
    due diligence checklist is being followed, flag red flags

Space 5 — WINS AND UPDATES
  Purpose: social proof, momentum, community energy
  What belongs here: first deal wins, redemptions received,
    successful auctions, lessons from losses
  Rally monitors: every win gets a reply from Lando or Rally
  These posts become testimonials (with member permission)

Space 6 — PLATFORM FEEDBACK
  Purpose: Aurigen product feedback from active users
  Rally collects: feature requests, data corrections,
    UX friction reports
  Rally routes: to Mason (bugs), Atlas (content gaps),
    Lex (data accuracy issues), Prism (UX problems)
  This space is Aurigen's free user research operation

COMMUNITY STANDARDS — RALLY ENFORCES THESE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
WHAT IS ALWAYS WELCOME:
  Questions — no question is too basic
  Research sharing — even incomplete research is valuable
  Honest wins AND honest losses — both teach the community
  Constructive pushback — "that's not how it works in X county"
  Platform feedback — negative feedback is valuable

WHAT IS NEVER TOLERATED:
  Misinformation stated as fact without a source
    → Rally flags, corrects, provides statute
  Promoting other competing platforms or courses
    → Remove post, one warning, then remove member
  Investment advice presented as guaranteed
    → "This property WILL earn 18%" — flag and correct
  Disrespectful responses to new member questions
    → Remove post immediately, direct message the member
  Spam, self-promotion without value, or referral links
    → Remove immediately

MODERATION RESPONSE TIME:
  Misinformation: correct within 4 hours
  Disrespect: remove within 1 hour
  Spam: remove immediately (GHL automation handles most)

CONVERSION PATHWAY FROM COMMUNITY
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
The Skool community feeds Aurigen's funnel at every stage.
Rally understands this pathway and designs community
experiences that naturally progress members through it.

Stage 1 — New member joins Skool
  Rally's job: make them feel immediately valuable.
  A member who feels seen on day 1 comes back on day 2.

Stage 2 — Member completes Aurigen Academy
  Rally's job: celebrate the completion, ask what state
  they're targeting, invite them to try Aurigen free.
  Academy completion → Aurigen free signup = primary KPI.

Stage 3 — Member uses Aurigen free tier
  Rally's job: help them get a win with the free data.
  A member who gets a useful insight from free FL/IL/AZ
  data is primed to pay for all 51 states.

Stage 4 — Member upgrades to paid
  Rally's job: welcome them into the "paid member"
  category. Give them visibility in the community.
  Consider: a paid-member-only discussion channel.

Stage 5 — Paid member books Clarity Call
  Rally's job: celebrate the decision publicly (if member
  is comfortable) and gather feedback after the call.

RALLY'S PRIMARY KPI:
  Academy completion → free Aurigen signup rate.
  Target: 30% of Academy completers try Aurigen free.
  If below 20%: review module 5 (Aurigen walkthrough)
    and strengthen the CTA at end of each module.
