1. MASTER FTC DISCLOSURE LANGUAGE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

FULL DISCLOSURE (use in long-form content, email, blog):

    "I am an affiliate of the Aurigen County Intelligence
    Platform. If you purchase through my link, I earn a $100
    commission at no extra cost to you. My recommendation
    reflects my honest opinion, but you should know about
    this financial relationship before deciding."

SHORT DISCLOSURE (use in social media, DMs):

    "Affiliate link — I earn a commission if you purchase."

MINIMUM DISCLOSURE (use only when character count is severely
limited, e.g., X/Twitter):

minimum only when format constraints make the full version
impractical. The disclosure must ALWAYS appear BEFORE or
immediately adjacent to the affiliate link — never buried
below, after, or hidden.

3. DIRECT MESSAGE DISCLOSURE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

RULE: The FTC requires disclosure in private messages, DMs,
and texts — not just public posts. If you share your
affiliate link in any one-on-one or group message, you must
disclose the financial relationship in that same message.

    looking into.

    [AFFILIATE LINK]

    either way.


TEMPLATE — SHORT DM:

    Full transparency — this is my affiliate link and I earn
    a commission if you purchase. No extra cost to you.

    [AFFILIATE LINK]


TEMPLATE — TEXT MESSAGE:

    if you sign up, no extra cost to you):
    [AFFILIATE LINK]

NOTE: The disclosure must be in the SAME message as the link.
Do not send the link in one message and the disclosure in a
separate follow-up.

4. EMAIL OUTREACH DISCLOSURE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

RULE: Disclosure must appear BEFORE the affiliate link in
the email body. It must be visible — not hidden in a footer
or fine print.

CAN-SPAM REQUIREMENTS:
- Email must include your real name or business name
- Email must include a physical mailing address
- Email must include a clear unsubscribe mechanism
- Subject line must not be deceptive
- Email must be identified as an advertisement if it is one

5. VIDEO / AUDIO CONTENT DISCLOSURE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

RULE: For video and audio content (YouTube, podcast,
Instagram Reels, TikTok, live streams), the disclosure must
be SPOKEN ALOUD during the content — not just in the
description or show notes. The FTC has been clear: if the
endorsement happens in the audio/video, the disclosure must
too.

TIMING: Disclose BEFORE or DURING your recommendation. Do
not wait until the end of a long video.


VERBAL SCRIPT — STANDARD (YouTube / Podcast):

    "Before I share this — full disclosure: I'm an affiliate
    for the Aurigen platform, which means I earn a
    commission if you sign up through my link. That doesn't
    change the price for you. I want to be upfront about
    that so you can weigh my recommendation accordingly."


VERBAL SCRIPT — SHORT (TikTok / Reels / Stories):

    "Quick disclosure — this is an affiliate link and I earn
    a commission if you sign up. Doesn't cost you extra."


VERBAL SCRIPT — PODCAST MID-ROLL:

    "I want to mention the Aurigen County Intelligence
    Platform — and in the interest of transparency, I'm an
    affiliate, so I do earn a commission if you use my link.
    I wouldn't mention it if I didn't think it was worth
    your time, but I want you to know that upfront."


VIDEO DESCRIPTION / SHOW NOTES (in addition to verbal):

    DISCLOSURE: This video contains an affiliate link.

6. AFFILIATE AGREEMENT SUMMARY
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

The following terms apply to all Aurigen affiliates. By
accepting an affiliate link, the affiliate agrees to these
terms in full.


A) MANDATORY FTC DISCLOSURE

    Affiliate must include an FTC-compliant disclosure on
    EVERY promotion — public post, private message, email,
    video, audio, or any other medium — that contains or
    references their affiliate link. No exceptions.

    Approved disclosure language is provided in this
    document. Affiliates may rephrase in their own voice
    provided the meaning is preserved: the audience must
    clearly understand that (1) the affiliate has a
    financial relationship with Aurigen, and (2) the
    affiliate earns a commission from purchases made
    through their link.


B) NO INCOME CLAIMS

    Affiliate must NOT make income claims of any kind,
    including but not limited to:

    - "I made $X with this"
    - "You'll earn $X"
    - "This pays for itself"
    - "I've already made my money back"
    - Sharing commission earnings screenshots
    - Implying that purchasing the platform leads to any
      specific financial outcome

    This applies to both explicit and implied income claims.


C) NO GUARANTEED RETURN CLAIMS

    Affiliate must NOT make claims or implications that
    tax lien or tax deed investing guarantees returns,
    including but not limited to:

    - "Guaranteed 8-36% returns"
    - "You can't lose money on tax liens"
    - "This is a safe investment"
    - "Risk-free"
    - Any specific return percentages presented as guaranteed

    Tax lien and tax deed investing carries real risk.
    Affiliates must not misrepresent this.


D) NO MISREPRESENTATION OF THE PLATFORM

    Affiliate must NOT:

    - Claim the platform provides investment advice
    - Claim the platform guarantees investment success
    - Misrepresent the platform's features or capabilities
    - Claim the platform replaces professional legal, tax,
      or financial counsel
    - Fabricate testimonials or results
    - Use fake scarcity or urgency tactics (e.g., "price
      going up tonight" unless Aurigen has officially
      announced a price change)


E) NO SPAM OR DECEPTIVE PRACTICES

    Affiliate must NOT:

    - Send unsolicited bulk email or messages
    - Use misleading subject lines or message previews
    - Post affiliate links in spaces where advertising is
      prohibited without permission
    - Use bait-and-switch tactics
    - Create fake accounts or personas to promote
    - Use deceptive ad copy or clickbait that
      misrepresents what the user will receive
    - Bid on "Aurigen" branded keywords in paid search
      without written permission


F) VIOLATION CONSEQUENCES

    Violation of any of the above terms results in:

    1. Immediate termination of affiliate status
    2. Deactivation of affiliate link
    3. Forfeiture of all unpaid commissions
    4. Potential legal action if the violation exposes
       Aurigen to regulatory liability

    Aurigen may, at its sole discretion, issue a warning
    for minor first-time violations, but is under no
    obligation to do so.


G) CONTENT AUDIT RIGHTS

    Aurigen reserves the right to:

    - Review any affiliate content at any time
    - Request removal or modification of non-compliant
      content within 24 hours of notice
    - Monitor affiliate link usage and traffic sources
    - Require affiliates to submit content for pre-approval
      if prior violations have occurred

    Affiliates agree to cooperate promptly with any audit
    or content review request.
