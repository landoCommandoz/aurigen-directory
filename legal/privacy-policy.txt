1. INTRODUCTION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

This Privacy Policy describes how Landon Brewington [pending LLC formation]
("Aurigen," "we," "us," "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your
information when you access or use the Aurigen County Intelligence Platform
("the Platform," "the Service") located at aurigendirectory.com and any
associated subdomains. By using the Platform, you consent to the data practices
described in this Privacy Policy.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our
data practices. If you do not agree with the practices described in this
Privacy Policy, please do not use the Platform.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

    (a) Email Address. When you register for free or paid access to the
        Platform, we collect your email address via a gate form hosted on
        Netlify Forms. This is the only piece of personally identifiable
        information we collect directly.

    (b) Payment Information. When you purchase full access, your payment is
        processed entirely by Stripe, Inc. Stripe collects your payment card
        number, expiration date, CVV, and billing information. We never see,
        receive, store, or have access to your full card number, CVV, or
        billing details. We may receive from Stripe a transaction confirmation,
        the last four digits of your card, and your email address for order
        fulfillment purposes.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

    (a) localStorage Data. The Platform uses your browser's localStorage to
        store access preferences, language settings, investor profile data
        (DNA archetype selections), and access tier flags. This data is stored
        locally on your device, is never transmitted to our servers or any
        third party, and contains no personally identifiable information.

    (b) Server Logs. Netlify, our hosting provider, may automatically collect
        standard server log data, including your IP address, browser type,
        referring URL, and pages visited. This data is collected and retained
        by Netlify in accordance with Netlify's own privacy policy and is not
        directly accessed or used by us for tracking or profiling purposes.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

    We do NOT collect or store: passwords, Social Security numbers, government
    identification numbers, physical addresses, phone numbers, financial
    account numbers, or any biometric data.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

    (a) Platform Access. To deliver your access credentials and manage your
        access to the Platform.

    (b) Communications. To send you educational content, investor tips,
        product updates, new feature announcements, and information about the
        Aurigen platform and related services.

    (c) Transaction Fulfillment. To process your purchase and confirm your
        payment status.

    (d) Service Improvement. To understand aggregate usage patterns and
        improve the Platform. We do not build individual user profiles for
        advertising purposes.

    (e) Legal Compliance. To comply with applicable laws, regulations, or
        legal processes.

4. THIRD-PARTY SERVICE PROVIDERS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

We use the following third-party service providers to operate the Platform.
Each provider processes data in accordance with its own privacy policy:

    (a) Netlify (Hosting and Forms)
        Purpose: Website hosting, serverless functions, and form submissions.
        Data shared: Your email address (submitted via Netlify Forms), server
        log data (collected automatically by Netlify).
        Privacy policy: https://www.netlify.com/privacy/

    (b) Stripe, Inc. (Payment Processing)
        Purpose: Processing one-time payments for full Platform access.
        Data shared: Payment card information, billing details, and email
        address — all collected and processed directly by Stripe. We never
        see or store your full payment card details.
        Privacy policy: https://stripe.com/privacy

    (c) Google Fonts (Typography)
        Purpose: Loading typefaces used in the Platform's user interface.
        Data shared: When you load the Platform, your browser makes a request
        to Google's font servers, which may receive your IP address and
        browser information.
        Privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

    (d) GoHighLevel (Email Marketing and CRM)
        Purpose: Managing email lists and sending marketing, educational, and
        transactional emails.
        Data shared: Your email address.
        Privacy policy: https://www.gohighlevel.com/privacy-policy

    (e) Anthropic (AI Features)
        Purpose: Powering the AI advisor feature within the Platform.
        Data shared: The text of queries you submit to the AI advisor. No
        personally identifiable information is sent to Anthropic.
        Privacy policy: https://www.anthropic.com/privacy

4.2 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

    We do not sell, rent, lease, or trade your personal information to any
    third party for any purpose, including marketing or advertising. This
    commitment applies to all users, regardless of jurisdiction.

5. COOKIES AND LOCAL STORAGE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    (a) Cookies. The Platform does not set first-party tracking cookies. We
        do not use advertising cookies, retargeting pixels, or behavioral
        tracking technologies. Third-party services (such as Netlify or
        Stripe) may set their own cookies in accordance with their respective
        privacy policies.

    (b) localStorage. The Platform uses your browser's localStorage mechanism
        to store the following non-personal data on your device:
        - Language preference (English or Spanish)
        - Access tier status (free or paid)
        - Investor DNA archetype selections
        - UI preferences (e.g., collapsed/expanded panel states)

        This data remains on your device, is not transmitted to our servers,
        and can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser's site data.

    (c) No Third-Party Tracking. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook
        Pixel, or any third-party analytics or advertising tracking tools.

6. DATA RETENTION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    (a) Email addresses are retained for as long as you maintain access to
        the Platform, or until you request deletion.

    (b) Payment records are retained by Stripe in accordance with Stripe's
        data retention policies and applicable financial regulations.

    (c) localStorage data persists on your device until you clear your
        browser data or uninstall your browser.

    (d) Upon a valid deletion request, we will remove your email address from
        our active databases and instruct our third-party processors to do
        the same within 30 days, subject to any legal retention obligations.
