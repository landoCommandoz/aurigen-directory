1. INTRODUCTION

This Privacy Policy describes how Landon Brewington ("we," "us," "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Aurigen County Resource Directory ("the Directory," "the Service") located at aurigen-directory.netlify.app. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect the following information:

(a) Email Address. When you sign up for free or paid access, we collect your email address. This is the only piece of personal information we collect directly.

(b) We do NOT collect or store passwords, payment card numbers, billing addresses, or other sensitive financial data. All payment processing is handled entirely by Stripe (see Section 4).

3. HOW WE USE YOUR EMAIL

Your email address is used for the following purposes:

(a) Product Access. To deliver your access code and manage your access to the Directory.

(b) Investor Tips and Educational Content. To send you educational emails about tax lien and tax deed investing.

(c) Product Updates. To notify you of updates, new features, or changes to the Directory.

You may opt out of marketing emails at any time (see Section 6).

4. THIRD-PARTY SERVICE PROVIDERS

We use the following third-party processors to operate the Service:

(a) Stripe (Payment Processing). Stripe handles all payment transactions. When you make a purchase, your payment card data is collected and processed entirely by Stripe. We never see, access, or store your card number, CVV, or billing details. Stripe's privacy policy is available at https://stripe.com/privacy.

(b) GoHighLevel (Email Marketing and CRM). We use GoHighLevel to manage our email list and send marketing and educational emails. Your email address is stored in GoHighLevel for this purpose. GoHighLevel's privacy policy is available at https://www.gohighlevel.com/privacy-policy.

(c) Anthropic (AI Advisor). The Directory includes an AI-powered advisor feature. Queries submitted to the AI advisor are processed by Anthropic. No personally identifiable information (PII) is sent to Anthropic — only the text of your query.

5. DATA RETENTION

Your email address is retained in our systems for as long as you maintain access to the Directory, or until you request deletion. Upon a valid deletion request, we will remove your email address from our active databases and instruct our third-party processors to do the same, subject to any legal retention requirements.

6. YOUR RIGHTS

You have the following rights regarding your data:

(a) Access. You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you by emailing support@theaurigen.com.

(b) Deletion. You may request that we delete your email address and associated data by emailing support@theaurigen.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

(c) Opt-Out of Marketing. Every marketing email we send includes an unsubscribe link. You may opt out of marketing emails at any time by clicking that link. Opting out of marketing emails does not affect transactional emails related to your account access.

(d) Correction. You may request correction of inaccurate data by contacting us.

7. COOKIES AND LOCAL STORAGE

(a) The Directory uses browser localStorage to maintain session state, such as your language preference and access status. This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any third-party tracking service.

(b) We do NOT use third-party tracking cookies.

(c) We do NOT use advertising pixels or retargeting technologies.

8. CAN-SPAM COMPLIANCE

In compliance with the CAN-SPAM Act:

(a) Every email we send includes a clear and conspicuous unsubscribe link.

(b) Unsubscribe requests are honored within 10 business days.

(c) Every email includes our physical mailing address.

(d) We do not use deceptive subject lines or false header information.

9. GDPR — EUROPEAN UNION USERS

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, we respect your rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

(a) Legal Basis for Processing. We process your email address on the basis of your consent (provided at sign-up) and for the performance of our contract with you (delivering the product you purchased).

(b) Data Subject Rights. You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, object to processing, and request portability of your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, email support@theaurigen.com.

(c) Data Transfers. Your data may be processed in the United States. By using the Service, you consent to the transfer of your data to the United States.

(d) We will respond to all GDPR-related requests within 30 days.

10. CHILDREN

The Directory is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 18, we will promptly delete that information.

11. DATA SECURITY

We take reasonable measures to protect your email address from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

12. CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Your continued use of the Service after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
