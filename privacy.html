Who We Are

Aurigen County Intelligence Platform — aurigendirectory.com

We build tools that help real estate investors research tax lien and tax deed auctions across all 50 U.S. states and Washington, D.C.

What We Collect

  • Email address (when you sign up for free or paid access)
  • Payment information (processed by Stripe — we never see or store your card number)
  • Usage data (which states and counties you view, searches you run)
  • Device and browser type

How We Use It

  • To give you access to the platform
  • To send The Aurigen Intelligence Brief (our free investor newsletter)
  • To improve the platform based on how people use it

We never sell your data. Ever.

Who We Share It With

  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Beehiiv — email delivery
  • Supabase — secure data storage

No advertisers. No data brokers. No exceptions.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Unsubscribe from any email at any time
  • Opt out of the sale of personal information (we don't sell it, but the right is yours under California law)

California residents: Under the CCPA, you have additional rights including the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete it, and the right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights. We do not sell personal information as defined by the CCPA.

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@aurigendirectory.com.

Cookies

We use essential cookies only — things like remembering your login status and language preference. No tracking cookies. No advertising cookies. No third-party analytics cookies.

Data Retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. If you request deletion, we remove your data within 30 days. Payment records may be retained longer as required by law.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we'll notify subscribers via email before the changes take effect.

Contact

Questions or concerns about your privacy? Reach us at privacy@aurigendirectory.com.
