## SECTION 3: AFFILIATE PROGRAM FTC DISCLOSURE LANGUAGE

### Required: Material Connection Disclosure

Under FTC Endorsement Guides (16 CFR Part 255), any person sharing a referral/affiliate link must disclose the material connection (financial compensation) clearly and conspicuously.

### Disclosure Language — Copy-Paste Ready:

---

**SHORT VERSION (for social media posts, DMs, and comments):**

> #ad I earn a commission if you purchase through my link. Aurigen is an educational research tool for tax lien and tax deed investors. Tax lien investing involves risk — always consult a qualified professional before investing.

---

**MEDIUM VERSION (for emails, blog posts, or longer-form content):**

> Disclosure: I am an affiliate of Aurigen and earn a commission on purchases made through my referral link. Aurigen County Intelligence Platform is an educational research tool — it does not provide investment, legal, or financial advice. Tax lien and tax deed investing involves risk, including potential loss of principal. Statutory rates shown are maximums and actual returns vary. Always conduct independent due diligence and consult a qualified professional before investing.

---

**LONG VERSION (for landing pages or dedicated review content):**

> Affiliate Disclosure: This page contains affiliate links. If you purchase Aurigen through my link, I receive a commission at no additional cost to you. This does not influence my opinion or review. Aurigen County Intelligence Platform is an educational research tool for tax lien and tax deed investors covering all 50 U.S. states plus the District of Columbia. It does not constitute investment, legal, or financial advice. Tax lien and tax deed investing involves substantial risk, including potential loss of your entire investment. Rates shown reflect statutory maximums — actual returns depend on competitive bidding, redemption outcomes, and county-specific conditions. Past performance does not guarantee future results. Always conduct your own due diligence and consult a qualified legal, tax, or financial professional before making any investment decisions.

---

### Disclosure Placement Rules:

1. **Social media posts:** Disclosure must appear BEFORE the link, not after. Use #ad or "Paid partnership" at the beginning of the post.
2. **Stories/Reels:** Disclosure must be visible on-screen (not hidden behind "see more"). Use text overlay.
3. **DMs:** First mention of the link must include "I earn a commission if you purchase through this link."
4. **Email:** Disclosure must appear in the same email as the link, preferably near the link itself.
5. **Video content:** Verbal disclosure within the first 30 seconds AND written disclosure in video description.

### What Affiliates Must NEVER Say:

- "Guaranteed returns" or "guaranteed income"
- "Make $X per month" or any specific dollar amount as a projection
- "Risk-free" or "passive income guaranteed"
- "You will earn [X]%" — statutory rates are maximums, not guarantees
- "I made $X using this tool" (unless literally true and accompanied by appropriate disclaimers)
- Any claim that the directory is financial advice or a substitute for professional consultation

### What Affiliates CAN Say:

- "I use this tool for my tax lien research"
- "It covers all 51 jurisdictions with county-level data"
- "Statutory maximum rates vary by state — this shows you what's possible"
- "It's an educational platform, not financial advice"
- "$197 one-time — no monthly subscription"
