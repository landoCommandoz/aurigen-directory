## WHAT THE DIRECTORY IS

Aurigen County Intelligence Platform (aurigendirectory.com) is a research tool built for tax lien and tax deed investors. It covers all 51 U.S. jurisdictions (50 states + DC) with county-by-county data, investor scoring, legal classifications, auction calendars, and a suite of tools that help investors move from confusion to a clear first-deal strategy.

The person on your call already used the directory before booking. They have context. They are not a cold lead.

### What the free tier gives them:
- Full map — all 51 states visible, color-coded by investment type
- State detail cards for all 51 states — rate, redemption period, beginner score, risks, auction signup steps, OTC availability
- Three states with full access (Florida, Illinois, Arizona)
- Sage AI (limited) — basic Q&A about tax lien/deed investing

### What the paid tier ($197 one-time) adds:
- County-level directory for all 51 states — every county, auction platform, deposit info, direct links
- DNA Investor Profiler — archetype quiz that maps their risk tolerance, timeline, and budget to their best-fit states
- Versus — side-by-side state comparison tool
- Deal Analyzer — deal calculator with two-state compare
- Sage AI (full) — deeper Q&A with CTA cards and strategy guidance
- Scout Checklist — state-specific due diligence checklist generator
- Auctions Calendar — full auction feed with filters
- Pulse Alerts — personalized auction alert feed

The directory is a one-time $197 purchase. It is not a subscription. It is a research tool, not a course.

## THE UPGRADE PATH FROM DIRECTORY TO ADVISORY

Every person on a Clarity Call came through the directory. That means they have already demonstrated:
1. They are interested in tax lien/deed investing (not casually curious)
2. They are willing to spend money to learn more ($197 is a filter)
3. They have used at least some of the tools and self-qualified

The directory removes the education objection. They are not asking "what is a tax lien?" They are asking "what do I do next and how do I not make a mistake?"

That is the exact gap the advisory programs fill.

**The natural bridge:**
"You've already got the research tool. You know what states match your profile. The question now is how to actually deploy — and that's where we can do this together instead of you figuring it out alone."

## KEY TALKING POINTS

**On the directory itself:**
- "The directory gave you the data. The advisory program gives you the strategy on top of the data."
- "Most investors who come through the directory can see exactly which states match them — the challenge is knowing what to do once you're at the auction."
- "The DNA profiler in the platform matched you to [their states] for a reason. Let's talk about what's actually happening in those markets."

**On the advisory programs:**
- "This isn't a course. There are no videos to watch on your own schedule. This is us working your specific situation with you, on your timeline."
- "The 45-day program is structured around getting you to your first bid. That's the entire purpose — not more research, an actual bid."
- "The reason people work with us versus going it alone is accountability and deal review. You're not guessing whether a county is a good target — we've already done that research."

**On urgency (state this accurately):**
- Auction seasons in many states are on fixed calendars. Missing a registration window means waiting months for the next cycle.
- This is a real and verifiable urgency — do not manufacture urgency, but do communicate it accurately when it applies to their target states.

## WHAT NOT TO SAY — FTC COMPLIANCE

These are hard rules. No exceptions.

- Do not guarantee any return, yield, or income. Not "you can expect 16%" or "most clients make back their program fee." Nothing.
- Do not say "you will get your money back" in any form.
- Do not reference specific student results unless Lando has provided verified, documented testimonials with proper disclaimers.
- Do not say the program is "risk-free." Tax lien and deed investing carries real risk including loss of principal.
- Do not make up scarcity. Only reference program capacity limits if Lando has confirmed they are real.
- Do not reference specific seminar brands, educators, or programs by name.

If a caller asks "what kind of returns should I expect?" — the correct answer is:
"Returns vary significantly based on state, county, property type, and how well your due diligence is done. The goal of the program is to put you in a position to make informed decisions — not to promise you a specific outcome."
