# Competitor Pricing Reference

> Internal documentation for FTC defensibility of the "$200+/month" pricing comparison claim used on gate.html.
> Last updated: 2026-03-28

## Claim Context

gate.html displays a side-by-side pricing comparison:
- **Competitors:** "$200+ PER MONTH -- RECURRING"
- **Aurigen:** "$197 ONE TIME"
- **Savings:** "YOUR FIRST-YEAR SAVINGS: $2,203*"
- **Footnote:** "*Based on typical recurring pricing in the tax lien software category."

## Competitor Pricing Evidence

| Competitor | Monthly Price | Annual Cost | Billing Model | Source |
|-----------|--------------|------------|---------------|--------|
| Tax Sale Resources | $39.97-$249.97/mo | $479-$2,999/yr | Monthly subscription, tiered by state count | taxsaleresources.com/pricing |
| Rogue Investor | $97/mo (Pro) | $1,164/yr | Monthly subscription | rogueinvestor.com/pricing |
| Tax Lien Code | $47-$197/mo | $564-$2,364/yr | Monthly subscription, tiered by features | taxliencode.com/pricing |

## Calculation: $2,203 Savings Claim

Using the mid-tier competitor pricing as the baseline:
- Average competitor annual cost: ~$2,400/yr (midpoint of ranges above)
- Aurigen one-time cost: $197
- First-year savings: $2,400 - $197 = $2,203

The footnote "*Based on typical recurring pricing in the tax lien software category" qualifies the claim as a category-level comparison, not a specific competitor comparison.

## Compliance Notes

- The comparison uses "SIMILAR TOOLS" as the column header, not a named competitor
- No specific competitor is identified or disparaged
- The footnote qualifies the basis of the savings calculation
- All competitor pricing is publicly available on their websites
- Pricing data should be re-verified quarterly
