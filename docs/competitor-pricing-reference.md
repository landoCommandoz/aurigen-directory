## Claim Context

gate.html displays a side-by-side pricing comparison:
- **Competitors:** "$200+ PER MONTH -- RECURRING"
- **Aurigen:** "$197 ONE TIME"
- **Savings:** "YOUR FIRST-YEAR SAVINGS: $2,203*"
- **Footnote:** "*Based on typical recurring pricing in the tax lien software category."

## Calculation: $2,203 Savings Claim

**Methodology:** Based on mid-tier multi-state plans (the tier most comparable to Aurigen's 51-state coverage):
- Tax Sale Resources multi-state plan: ~$200/mo ($2,400/yr)
- Rogue Investor Pro: $97/mo ($1,164/yr)
- Tax Lien Code multi-feature: ~$197/mo ($2,364/yr)
- **Baseline used:** $2,400/yr (mid-tier multi-state plan pricing)
- Aurigen one-time cost: $197
- First-year savings: $2,400 - $197 = $2,203

**Note:** The $2,400 baseline reflects the cost of multi-state access tiers comparable to Aurigen's 51-state coverage. Entry-level single-state plans are significantly cheaper but do not provide equivalent coverage.

The footnote "*Based on typical recurring pricing in the tax lien software category" qualifies the claim as a category-level comparison, not a specific competitor comparison.
