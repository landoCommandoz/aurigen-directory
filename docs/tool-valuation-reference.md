## Methodology

- Each tool is compared to the closest standalone equivalent available in the tax lien investing market
- Where no direct equivalent exists (DNA Profiler, Versus), we use the cost of the closest manual alternative
- All estimates use the lower end of comparable pricing ranges
- The "$1,500+" claim uses a rounded-down figure from the $1,550 total

## Compliance Notes

- The claim is presented as an internal valuation ("Total value if purchased separately"), not a market price
- It is qualified with "if purchased separately" -- acknowledging these are not bundled elsewhere
- The purpose is to contextualize the $197 price point, not to make a savings guarantee
- Conservative estimates are used throughout to avoid overstatement
