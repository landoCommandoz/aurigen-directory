## Status Key

- **Generated** — HTML file exists in `sites/`, not yet deployed
- **Deployed** — Live on Netlify, URL in `leads.csv`
- **Claimed** — Business owner took over the site
- **Down** — Site was live but is no longer accessible
- **Archived** — Intentionally taken down

## Notes
